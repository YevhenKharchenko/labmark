Game World

Beast Go! takes place in this crazy world where different animals have claimed their own territories, and trust me, getting around isn't always easy with all the weird terrain and random stuff that happens.

Every board's got its own vibe - you might be trekking through forests one game, then dealing with desert heat the next, or trying not to slip on ice plains.

And there are always secret paths and surprises hiding everywhere.

How to Play

  • Roll or choose movement options to advance across board tiles

  • Land on special tiles to trigger events and power-ups

  • Recruit animal companions with unique abilities

  • You've gotta think ahead about which way to go and what moves to make

  • Beat everyone else to the finish line while grabbing as much good stuff as you can along the way

Strategy Elements

  • Tactical movement choices

  • Animal companions with special abilities

  • Interactive board events

  • Bonus encounters and loot tiles

  • Risks and rewards balance on every turn
